I research corporate finance in emerging markets — how liquidity, risk, and investment decisions shape the way firms pay out to shareholders. Most of my work uses survey data and structural equation modeling to test these relationships empirically, with a current focus on Nepal's banking sector.

The model behind my current published paper — liquidity and risk affect dividend policy both directly and through investment.
